How W Studio Learn handles student account data, course access, checkout records, and support information.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to W Studio Learn at learn.wstudio3d.com. The platform provides online courses, student login, digital course access, and checkout through third-party services.

By using the site, creating an account, or buying a course, you understand that your information may be processed to provide the service, protect access, and support your purchase.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information such as name, email address, login provider, and Firebase user ID.
  • Course access records such as course ID, purchase status, PayPal order ID, payment amount, and access dates.
  • Support information you send by email, including your message, contact details, and order references.
  • Technical information handled by service providers, such as device data, IP address, security logs, and usage events needed to operate the site.
  • Language preference stored in your browser so the site can remember EN, DE, or ES.

W Studio Learn does not store full payment card numbers. PayPal processes payment details directly.

3. How we use information

  • To create and secure student accounts.
  • To unlock purchased courses and show your student dashboard.
  • To process checkout, verify payment status, and keep purchase records.
  • To answer support requests, resolve access issues, and handle refunds when eligible.
  • To protect the platform from fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, or technical failures.
  • To improve course delivery, navigation, language support, and user experience.

4. Service providers

The platform uses trusted service providers to operate internationally. These may include Firebase and Google services for authentication and database records, Vercel for hosting and serverless APIs, PayPal for checkout, and YouTube or YouTube-nocookie embeds for video delivery.

These providers may process data in countries different from yours. W Studio Learn uses them only to run the platform, deliver courses, process payments, and provide support.

5. Retention and security

Account and purchase records are kept as long as needed to provide course access, meet business and tax needs, resolve disputes, and protect the platform. You can request account review or deletion, but some purchase records may need to be kept where required for legitimate business or legal reasons.

W Studio Learn uses passwordless provider security, Firebase Authentication, server-side token checks, protected environment variables, and restricted database rules to reduce risk.

6. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, object to, restrict, or delete personal information. You may also contact us about privacy questions or course access records connected to your email.

You can choose not to create an account, but paid course access requires an account so purchases can be attached to a student profile.

7. Children

W Studio Learn is designed for entrepreneurs, freelancers, creators, and business learners. It is not intended for children under 13, and students under the age required by their local law should use the platform only with appropriate parent or guardian permission.
