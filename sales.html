Questions, answered

Before you begin.

Are all courses available in three languages?

Yes. The platform provides English, German and Spanish versions for the catalog, lessons, practical guidance and final assessments.

Can an institution offer only selected courses?

Contact W Studio Learn with your audience and curriculum goals. We can discuss a selected-course path or the complete 14-course program.

What must a student do to earn the Pro certificate?

Students must have active access, complete every lesson and pass all 14 final exams with a score of at least 70%.

Can freelancers enroll independently?

Yes. Independent learners can choose individual courses from the catalog and learn at their own pace.
